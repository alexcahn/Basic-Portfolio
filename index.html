About Me

My name is Alex Cahn and I am originally from Irvine, California. I graduated from California State University, Chico with a degree in Marketing and a minor in Sales. After college, I got my first job ever at a company called Glassdoor. At Glassdoor, I watched the company grow from 80 employees to 1,000 worldwide and eventually moved to Chicago to help start their office. A few years later, I wanted to be back at a startup and currently work at G2 Crowd. This Bootcamp is exciting to me because it's going to help me boost my current skillset as well as help me figure out which career course I'd like to take.

People always ask me, "why the hell would you move to Chicago from California?" Well, the truth is, I'm hoping to make my way back there at some point in my life. For now, the beer and food in this city is too damn good!

Favorite sports teams include the Raiders, Ducks and Angels. Favorite bands include Red Hot Chili Peppers, Led Zeppelin and Metallica. Favorite video game of all time is Madden.
